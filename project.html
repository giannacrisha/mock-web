Select a project


Click on a project in the side bar to view its description and other details. Click on it again to exit or click on another project to view another project. A new tab containing the project will open when you click on the thumbnail. You can also visit the repositories of these project by click on the title of the project.



All of these projects are created from scratch with HTML, CSS, and a little bit of Javascript.For more information, you can reach out to me by going to the Contact tab and sending me an email through the contact form. You can also reach out to me on my social media accounts. I look forward to collaborating with you!
